# CONTEXT DOCUMENT — CENTRO VETERINÁRIO DE AVEIRO
### Website Discovery & Brand Research Briefing for an AI Web Design Agent

> **Purpose of this document.** This is a complete discovery brief compiled from all publicly available information about *Centro Veterinário de Aveiro* (CVA). It is written so that a second AI agent — or a professional design studio — can design and build an exceptional website **without conducting further research**. Every material fact is attributed to a source. Where information could not be confirmed publicly, it is explicitly labelled **"Not publicly available"** and, where useful, a clearly-marked *assumption* or *recommendation* is offered in its place.
>
> **Compiled:** 17 July 2026.
> **A note on confidence.** CVA is a small, long-established suburban clinic with a strong social-media presence but **no functioning official website** at the time of research. This means a large share of "hard" web data (site structure, SEO, on-page content) does not exist yet — which is precisely the opportunity this document is preparing for. Facts are drawn from Facebook, Instagram, Google Business Profile, Portuguese business directories, and a 2020 trade-magazine feature (*Veterinária Atual*) that profiled the clinic and its founder in depth.

# BUSINESS PROFILE

### Company Name
**Centro Veterinário de Aveiro** (commonly abbreviated **CVA**). *(S1, S2)*

### Legal Name
Not publicly available in the sources reviewed (no NIPC/registered *firma* was surfaced). The Páginas Amarelas and postal directories list the trading name only. *(S6, S7)* — **Recommendation:** confirm the legal entity and NIPC directly with the client before adding legal footer / schema data.

### Business Type
Independent, owner-operated **veterinary clinic** — explicitly described by its founder as a **"clínica mista" (mixed practice)**: companion animals *and* production animals, with a historical specialism in **dairy-cattle** herd work. *(S1)*

### Industry
Veterinary services / animal healthcare (companion-animal primary care with production-animal consultancy). Portuguese CAE sector: veterinary activities (*atividades veterinárias*). *(S1)*

### Description
Centro Veterinário de Aveiro is a long-established, family-doctor-style veterinary clinic serving the city of Aveiro and surrounding suburban and rural areas. Founded in **2001** by veterinarian **Carlos Ribeiro** in a purpose-designed **~160 m²** space, it was conceived from the outset for medical *and* surgical diagnosis and treatment of companion animals, while retaining assistance to production animals and dairy herds. Over two decades it grew from a suburban practice into what its director calls *"a consolidated, consensual and recognised veterinary clinic"* known not just in its suburb but across the city and neighbouring localities. It positions itself with the phrase **"Somos o médico de família do seu animal"** ("We are your animal's family doctor"). *(S1, S2)*

### Mission
No formally published mission statement exists on an official website (none is live). The *de facto* mission, drawn consistently from its own channels, is:

> *"Promote the health and wellbeing of animals"* — treating **"every animal as part of the family, because it truly is."** *(S3, S4)*

The founder's stated philosophy: deliver **generalist medicine done excellently**, refer complex cases to reference hospitals rather than over-promising in-house specialisation, and build a business on **retained, loyal, well-served clients** rather than price-cutting. *(S1)*

### Values
Synthesised from primary sources *(S1, S2, S3)*:

- **Dedication and professionalism** ("dedicação e profissionalismo") — its own standing tagline.
- **Continuity of care** — the "family doctor" framing; accompanying an animal through every life stage.
- **Honesty over discounting** — the founder is openly critical of clinics that compete on "unacceptable prices"/dumping and prizes fair pricing for fair work.
- **Clinical rigour** — investment in diagnostic technology and referral partnerships for definitive diagnoses.
- **Warmth and client relationship** — belief that the first five minutes of human contact define how a client judges the clinic.
- **Animal welfare first** — recurring message across Instagram.

### Target Customers
- **Primary:** Pet owners (dogs, cats) in Aveiro city, its suburbs (notably the Alagoas / Esgueira axis) and neighbouring localities. *(S1)*
- **Secondary:** Owners of **exotics** — rabbits, guinea pigs and other small mammals (a stated competence of Dr. Dália Castro). *(S1, S3)*
- **Tertiary / legacy:** **Production-animal and dairy-farm** operators in the Aveiro district — a founding line of business and still served, primarily via the director's morning herd rounds. *(S1)*
- **Behavioural profile:** Values-driven, loyalty-prone owners who treat pets as family; a segment willing to pay fair prices for trust and continuity rather than chase the cheapest option. Also price-sensitive shoppers reached through the clinic's thrice-yearly **sterilisation campaigns**. *(S1)*

### Unique Selling Proposition
**"Your animal's family doctor" — 35+ years of hands-on clinical and surgical experience in one trusted, independent, non-corporate neighbourhood clinic.** In a market increasingly dominated by international chains (AniCura/Mars, Onevet), CVA's differentiator is **personal continuity of care**: the same experienced veterinarians know your animal across its whole life, backed by a deliberate referral network for anything requiring true specialisation. *(S1, S2)*

### Years in Operation
**Founded 2001** → **~25 years** as of 2026. *(S1: "inaugurado o CVA … em 2001".)* Note the founder's individual professional experience dates to **1979**; the clinic's marketing "+35 years of clinical and surgical practice" refers to the **founder's career**, not the clinic's age. # TECHNICAL RECOMMENDATIONS

- **CMS:** For a small clinic that must self-manage content and campaigns, recommend **WordPress** (mature PT ecosystem, easy handover) **or** a modern JAMstack option (**Astro/Next.js + a lightweight headless CMS**) if performance is prioritised. For fastest maintenance-light delivery, **Webflow** is a strong alternative. Pick based on who maintains it post-launch.
- **Hosting:** Fast EU-based hosting (Portugal/Spain/Frankfurt edge) for low latency to local users; CDN in front. Providers: Cloudflare (CDN/DNS + Pages), or a managed WP host with EU data centres. Ensure HTTPS, HTTP/2/3.
- **SEO structure:** Clean URL scheme (see SEO section), one H1/page, semantic headings, breadcrumb nav, XML sitemap, robots.txt, canonical tags, hreflang only if adding an English version.
- **Performance improvements:** Target **Core Web Vitals green** — LCP < 2.5s, INP < 200ms, CLS < 0.1. Lazy-load images, preconnect fonts, minimal JS, static rendering where possible.
- **Accessibility improvements:** **WCAG 2.1 AA** — colour contrast ≥ 4.5:1, focus states, alt text (in PT), semantic landmarks, keyboard nav, `prefers-reduced-motion`, min 44px tap targets, accessible forms with labels.
- **Schema.org:** Implement **`VeterinaryCare`** (subtype of `LocalBusiness` + `MedicalBusiness`) with name, address, geo, `openingHoursSpecification`, telephone, priceRange, `sameAs` (Facebook/Instagram), plus **`FAQPage`**, **`BreadcrumbList`**, and **`Review`/`AggregateRating`** markup.
- **Local SEO improvements:** Claim/optimise **Google Business Profile** (categories: Veterinarian + Emergency veterinarian service, photos, services, posts, Q&A); **fix NAP consistency** across Páginas Amarelas, ciberforma, chequeveterinario, Bing Places, Apple Maps; seed reviews to grow Google's 22 toward Facebook's volume; embed Google Map.
- **Core Web Vitals:** Image CDN + `srcset`, modern formats (AVIF/WebP), font-display swap, critical CSS inline, defer non-critical JS.
- **Image optimization:** Compress the existing photo library; consistent aspect ratios; descriptive PT alt text (SEO + accessibility).
- **Security:** HTTPS everywhere, HSTS, secure contact-form (spam/honeypot + reCAPTCHA v3), GDPR-compliant cookie consent (privacy-first defaults), a **Política de Privacidade** page, and a **branded email** on the domain. Keep CMS/plugins updated; daily backups.
- **Integrations to consider:** WhatsApp click-to-chat, appointment form → email/WhatsApp (or a booking tool), Meta Pixel (with consent) for retargeting the strong social audience.
